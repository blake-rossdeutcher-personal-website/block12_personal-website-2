When I am not making amazing web pages, I enjoy:

Climbing

I started rock climbing at the rec center at the University of Iowa. After graduation, I stayed in the Chicago suburbs, where I grew up, before moving to North Carolina to pursue rock climbing. While living there, a friend brought me on trips to Colorado and New Hampshire to introduce me to ice. I now live in Colorado and have aspirations to climb to the summit of Denali in Alaska.

Skiing

I started skiing after college when I visited a friend who lived in Denver. Because I already knew ice skating, it was easy to learn. I skied in North Carolina, but it was icy. To catch some fresh pow, I moved to Colorado and now, I shred the gnar.

Playing Guitar

I tried teaching myself guitar in Iowa, but never made it very far, so I sold it. While living in North Carolina, I bought a used guitar and started teaching myself. One of my friends is teaching me and I am very excited to learn. I played in front of other people for the first time at a campfire in Utah.
